## üìù Tips for Recording

1. **Speak slowly and clearly** - Pretend you're talking to a real client
2. **Pause between sections** - Give viewers time to absorb what they're seeing
3. **Use "you" and "your"** - Make it personal ("your clients," "your practice")
4. **Avoid jargon** - No "components," "React," "Tailwind"‚Äîuse "section," "button," "layout"
5. **Show, don't just tell** - Actually click buttons, expand accordions, scroll smoothly
6. **Smile while talking** - It comes through in your voice!
7. **Test your audio** - Make sure your microphone is clear
8. **Practice once** - Do a quick dry run to time yourself
